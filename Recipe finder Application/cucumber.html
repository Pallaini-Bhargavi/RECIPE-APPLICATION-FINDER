Cucumber Raita Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup yogurt (curd)
  • 1 medium cucumber, grated or finely chopped
  • 1/4 tsp roasted cumin powder
  • 1/4 tsp black salt (optional)
  • 1/4 tsp regular salt (to taste)
  • 1 tbsp finely chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/2 tsp finely chopped green chilies (optional)
  • 1 pinch red chili powder (for garnish)

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Cucumber: Wash, peel, and grate the cucumber. Squeeze out excess water if necessary.
  2. Mix the Ingredients: In a bowl, whisk the yogurt until smooth. Add the grated cucumber, roasted cumin powder, black salt, regular salt, coriander leaves, and green chilies (if using).
  3. Combine Well: Mix all the ingredients thoroughly to combine the flavors.
  4. Garnish: Sprinkle a pinch of red chili powder and some more roasted cumin powder on top for garnish.
  5. Serve: Serve chilled as a side dish with biryani, pulao, or parathas.

Tip: For extra flavor, you can add a handful of grated carrots or finely chopped onions to the raita.
