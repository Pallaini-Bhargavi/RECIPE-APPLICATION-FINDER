Sprouts Salad Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mixed sprouts (moong, chana, etc.)
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped cucumber
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped tomato
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion (optional)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp chaat masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper powder
  • 1 tbsp finely chopped coriander leaves

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Sprouts: Wash the sprouts and steam them lightly for 5-7 minutes if preferred. Allow them to cool.
  2. Combine Vegetables: In a large mixing bowl, add the steamed sprouts, cucumber, tomato, and onion.
  3. Season: Add lemon juice, chaat masala, salt, and black pepper powder. Mix well.
  4. Garnish: Add freshly chopped coriander leaves for flavor and color.
  5. Serve: Serve the salad immediately or chill in the refrigerator for a refreshing experience.

Tip: You can add pomegranate seeds or boiled corn kernels for an extra crunch and sweetness.
