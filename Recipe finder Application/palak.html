Palak (Spinach) Soup Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh spinach leaves (palak), washed and chopped
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped tomato
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice (optional)
  • Fresh cream for garnish (optional)

Steps:

  1. Prepare Vegetables: Heat ghee or oil in a pot. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  2. Add Tomatoes and Spices: Add chopped tomatoes and cook for 2-3 minutes. Then add ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder, black pepper, and garam masala. Sauté for another minute.
  3. Cook Spinach: Add chopped spinach leaves and cook until they wilt, about 5 minutes.
  4. Simmer the Soup: Add water or vegetable broth, salt, and bring it to a boil. Lower the heat and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Blend the Soup: Let the soup cool slightly. Then blend it to a smooth puree using a hand blender or regular blender.
  6. Serve: Pour the soup back into the pot, bring it to a gentle simmer, and adjust seasoning if needed. Garnish with a swirl of fresh cream and a squeeze of lemon juice if desired.
  7. Enjoy: Serve hot with toasted bread or as a light, healthy meal.

Tip: You can add a pinch of cumin powder or a dash of chili flakes for extra flavor. You may also add a little boiled potato to make the soup creamier.
