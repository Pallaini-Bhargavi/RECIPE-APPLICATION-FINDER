Mixed Vegetable Soup Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup finely chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped beans
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped cabbage
  • 1/4 cup sweet corn kernels
  • 4 cups water or vegetable stock
  • 2 tbsp cornflour (mixed with 1/4 cup water to make a slurry)
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped garlic
  • 1 tbsp oil or butter
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce (optional)
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Vegetables: Wash and finely chop all the vegetables.
  2. Sauté Garlic: Heat oil or butter in a pot. Add chopped garlic and sauté for a minute until fragrant.
  3. Add Vegetables: Add the carrots, beans, cabbage, and sweet corn. Sauté for 2-3 minutes on medium heat.
  4. Add Stock: Pour water or vegetable stock into the pot. Let it simmer for 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Add Cornflour Slurry: Mix cornflour with water to make a smooth slurry. Add it to the soup, stirring continuously to avoid lumps. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the soup thickens.
  6. Season: Add salt, black pepper powder, and soy sauce (if using). Mix well.
  7. Garnish and Serve: Turn off the heat. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.

Tip: You can add or substitute vegetables like broccoli, zucchini, or mushrooms for more variety.
