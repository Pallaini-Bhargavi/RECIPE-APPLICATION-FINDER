Masala Rice Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 cup peas (optional)
  • 1-2 green chilies, slit
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps:

  1. Prepare Rice: Wash and soak the basmati rice for 20 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat Oil: In a pan, heat oil or ghee. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Sauté Onions and Tomatoes: Add the chopped onions and sauté until golden brown. Then, add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  4. Add Spices: Add turmeric powder, garam masala, and red chili powder. Stir well to combine the spices with the tomatoes.
  5. Add Rice and Peas: Add the soaked and drained rice to the pan, followed by the peas. Stir gently to combine with the masala.
  6. Cook Masala Rice: Add water and salt. Bring it to a boil, reduce the heat, cover the pan, and cook for 15-20 minutes or until the rice is fully cooked and the water is absorbed.
  7. Serve: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with yogurt or any curry of your choice.

Tip: You can add fried onions or cashews on top for an extra crunch!
