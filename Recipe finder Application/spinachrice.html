Spinach Rice (Palak Rice) Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups fresh spinach (palak), chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1-2 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 cup peas (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps:

  1. Prepare Rice: Wash and soak the basmati rice for 20 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat Oil: In a pan, heat oil. Add cumin seeds and sauté for 30 seconds until they splutter.
  3. Sauté Onions and Tomatoes: Add the chopped onions and cook until golden brown. Then, add chopped tomatoes, green chilies, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and garam masala. Cook until the tomatoes soften.
  4. Cook Spinach: Add the chopped spinach (palak) and cook for a few minutes until the spinach wilts and reduces in size.
  5. Add Rice and Peas: Add the drained rice and peas (optional). Stir gently to combine.
  6. Cook Spinach Rice: Add water and salt. Bring it to a boil, then reduce the heat, cover the pan, and cook for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is fully cooked and the water is absorbed.
  7. Serve: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with yogurt or curry.

Tip: You can also add a few cashews or peanuts for a crunchy texture!
