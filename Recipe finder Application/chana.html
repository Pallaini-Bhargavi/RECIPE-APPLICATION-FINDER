Chana Salad Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup boiled chickpeas (chana)
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped cucumber
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onions
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped coriander leaves
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp chaat masala
  • 1/2 tsp roasted cumin powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper powder
  • Salt to taste

Steps:

  1. Boil Chickpeas: If using dried chickpeas, soak them overnight and pressure cook until soft. Drain excess water.
  2. Mix Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, add boiled chickpeas, chopped cucumber, tomatoes, onions, and coriander leaves.
  3. Season: Add lemon juice, chaat masala, roasted cumin powder, black pepper powder, and salt. Mix well.
  4. Serve: Serve the salad immediately for a fresh and healthy snack.

Tip: You can add pomegranate seeds or boiled corn for an extra burst of flavor and texture.
