Sambar Rice Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup rice
  • 1/4 cup toor dal (pigeon peas)
  • 1 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, beans, drumstick, etc.)
  • 1 tbsp sambar powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • 1-2 dried red chilies
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 cups water
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps:

  1. Cook Rice and Dal: Wash the rice and toor dal. Cook them together in a pressure cooker with 3 cups of water and a pinch of turmeric. Mash slightly and set aside.
  2. Cook Vegetables: In a pot, boil the chopped vegetables with a pinch of salt until tender.
  3. Prepare Sambar Base: Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, dried red chilies, and curry leaves. Let them splutter.
  4. Add Tamarind and Spices: Add tamarind paste, sambar powder, and turmeric powder. Mix well and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Combine: Add the boiled vegetables and their water to the sambar base. Mix in the cooked rice and dal. Stir well.
  6. Simmer: Add more water to adjust the consistency and let it simmer for 5-7 minutes. Add salt to taste.
  7. Garnish: Turn off the heat and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  8. Serve: Serve hot with papad or pickle on the side.

Tip: You can add a teaspoon of ghee on top before serving for extra flavor.
