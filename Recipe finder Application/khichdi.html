Khichdi Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup rice
  • 1/4 cup yellow moong dal (split lentils)
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 cup chopped vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, etc.)
  • 4 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper (optional)

Steps:

  1. Wash and Soak: Wash the rice and moong dal thoroughly and soak them for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Heat Ghee and Temper: Heat ghee in a pressure cooker. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add Vegetables and Spices: Add the chopped vegetables, turmeric powder, and sauté for a minute.
  4. Add Rice and Dal: Drain the soaked rice and dal. Add them to the cooker and sauté for another minute.
  5. Add Water and Cook: Pour 4 cups of water, add salt, and mix well. Close the lid and pressure cook for 3-4 whistles on medium heat.
  6. Release Pressure: Allow the pressure to release naturally. Open the lid and gently stir the khichdi.
  7. Serve: Serve hot with ghee, yogurt, or pickle on the side.

Tip: For a softer consistency, increase the water quantity to 5 cups.
