Oats Idli Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup oats, powdered
  • 1/2 cup semolina (rava)
  • 1 cup yogurt (curd)
  • 1/2 cup grated carrots
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped coriander leaves
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp oil

Steps:

  1. Roast and Prepare Oats: Dry roast the oats in a pan until light golden. Cool them and grind into a fine powder.
  2. Roast Semolina: In the same pan, lightly roast the semolina for 1-2 minutes. Set aside.
  3. Prepare Tempering: Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Add urad dal and saut√© until golden.
  4. Mix Ingredients: In a bowl, mix powdered oats, roasted semolina, curd, grated carrots, coriander leaves, and the prepared tempering. Add salt and mix well.
  5. Rest the Batter: Let the batter rest for 10-15 minutes. Adjust the consistency by adding water if needed.
  6. Add Baking Soda: Just before steaming, add baking soda to the batter and mix gently.
  7. Steam the Idlis: Grease the idli molds and pour the batter into them. Steam for 10-12 minutes or until cooked.
  8. Serve: Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.

Tip: You can add finely chopped green chilies or ginger for extra flavor.
