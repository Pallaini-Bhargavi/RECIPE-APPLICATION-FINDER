Curd Rice Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 cup plain yogurt (curd)
  • 1-2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1-2 tsp oil
  • 1-2 green chilies, slit
  • 1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 1-2 tbsp pomegranate seeds (optional)
  • For Tempering:
    • 1 tsp cumin seeds
    • 1/2 tsp urad dal (black gram lentils)
    • 1/2 tsp chana dal (split chickpeas)
    • 8-10 curry leaves

Steps:

  1. Prepare Rice: Cook the rice and let it cool down to room temperature.
  2. Mix Yogurt: In a bowl, whisk the yogurt until smooth. Add salt and turmeric powder to the yogurt and mix well.
  3. Prepare Tempering: Heat oil in a small pan, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, urad dal, chana dal, and curry leaves. Saut√© for a minute until they splutter and turn golden brown.
  4. Combine Rice and Yogurt: Add the cooked rice to the yogurt mixture. Mix gently to coat the rice with the yogurt.
  5. Add Tempering: Pour the prepared tempering over the curd rice and mix well. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and optional pomegranate seeds.
  6. Serve: Serve curd rice chilled or at room temperature. It can be served with pickle or a side of papad.

Tip: You can add grated carrots or cucumber for added flavor and texture.
