Mushroom Pulao Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1 onion, finely sliced
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1-inch piece cinnamon stick
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps:

  1. Prepare Rice: Wash and soak the basmati rice for 20-30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Sauté Spices: In a large pan, heat ghee or oil. Add cumin seeds, green chilies, bay leaf, cinnamon stick, and cloves. Sauté until fragrant.
  3. Cook Onions: Add the sliced onions and cook until they turn golden brown.
  4. Cook Mushrooms: Add the sliced mushrooms and sauté until they release water and soften, about 5-7 minutes.
  5. Add Rice: Add the soaked and drained rice, turmeric powder, and garam masala. Stir gently to coat the rice with the spices.
  6. Cook the Pulao: Add 2 cups of water and salt to taste. Bring to a boil, then lower the heat, cover, and cook for 15-20 minutes or until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed.
  7. Serve: Garnish the mushroom pulao with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with raita or curry.

Tip: You can add a splash of lemon juice just before serving for an extra zing!
