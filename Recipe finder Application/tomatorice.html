Tomato Rice Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1-2 green chilies, slit
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/4 cup peas (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • For Tempering:
    • 1 tsp cumin seeds
    • 1-2 cloves
    • 1-inch piece cinnamon stick

Steps:

  1. Prepare Rice: Wash and soak the basmati rice for 20 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Sauté Spices: In a large pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds, cloves, and cinnamon stick. Sauté until fragrant.
  3. Cook Onions and Tomatoes: Add the chopped onions and cook until golden brown. Add the chopped tomatoes, green chilies, turmeric, red chili powder, and garam masala. Cook until the tomatoes become soft and the oil separates from the masala.
  4. Add Rice: Add the soaked and drained rice to the pan and stir gently to coat the rice with the tomato mixture.
  5. Cook Tomato Rice: Add water, peas (optional), and salt to taste. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat, cover, and cook for 15-20 minutes or until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed.
  6. Serve: Garnish the tomato rice with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with yogurt or raita.

Tip: You can also add a few fried cashews or peanuts on top for an added crunch!
