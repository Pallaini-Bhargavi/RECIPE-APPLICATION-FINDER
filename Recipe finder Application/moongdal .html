Moong Dal Chilla Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup moong dal (yellow split lentils)
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped green chilies (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (for soaking and to make batter)
  • Oil for frying

Steps:

  1. Soak Moong Dal: Wash and soak moong dal in water for 2-3 hours. Drain the water completely after soaking.
  2. Make the Batter: Blend the soaked moong dal into a smooth batter using a little water. The consistency should be thick but pourable.
  3. Add Vegetables and Spices: In the batter, add chopped onions, tomatoes, green chilies, coriander leaves, cumin seeds, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  4. Cook the Chilla: Heat a non-stick pan and drizzle a little oil. Pour a ladleful of the batter onto the pan and spread it evenly into a circle.
  5. Flip and Cook: Cook on medium heat for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown. Flip gently and cook the other side.
  6. Serve: Once both sides are crispy, remove the chilla from the pan and serve hot with chutney or yogurt.

Tip: You can also add grated carrots, spinach, or any vegetables of your choice for extra nutrition.
