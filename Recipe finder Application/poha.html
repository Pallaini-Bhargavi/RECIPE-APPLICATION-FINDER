Vegetable Poha Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flattened rice (poha)
  • 1/4 cup green peas
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small carrot, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Steps:

  1. Rinse the Poha: Rinse poha in water until it becomes soft. Drain the water completely and set it aside.
  2. Prepare the Vegetables: Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Add onions, green chilies, and saut√© until onions are translucent.
  3. Add Vegetables: Add green peas, carrots, and turmeric powder. Cook until the vegetables are soft.
  4. Mix Poha: Add the softened poha to the pan. Sprinkle salt and mix gently to combine everything.
  5. Cook: Cover and cook on low heat for 2-3 minutes to let the flavors blend.
  6. Garnish and Serve: Turn off the heat. Add lemon juice and garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Serve hot!

Tip: You can add roasted peanuts or cashews for extra crunch.
