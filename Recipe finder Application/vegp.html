Vegetable Pulao Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1 onion, finely sliced
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, potato)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1-2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1-inch piece cinnamon stick
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 2-3 cardamom pods
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps:

  1. Prepare Rice: Wash and soak the basmati rice for 20-30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Sauté Spices: In a large pan, heat ghee or oil. Add cumin seeds, green chilies, bay leaf, cinnamon stick, cloves, and cardamom pods. Sauté until fragrant.
  3. Cook Onions: Add the sliced onions and cook until they turn golden brown.
  4. Add Vegetables: Add the mixed vegetables and sauté for 2-3 minutes. Add turmeric powder and garam masala. Stir to combine.
  5. Add Rice: Add the soaked and drained rice, stirring gently to coat the rice with the spices.
  6. Cook the Pulao: Add 2 cups of water and salt to taste. Bring to a boil. Lower the heat, cover, and cook for 15-20 minutes or until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed.
  7. Serve: Garnish the pulao with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with raita or curry.

Tip: You can also add a few saffron strands soaked in milk for an aromatic touch.
