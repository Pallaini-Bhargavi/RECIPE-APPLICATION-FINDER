Coconut Rice Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1/2 cup fresh grated coconut
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1/4 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1-2 green chilies, slit
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/4 cup cashews or peanuts (optional)
  • 1-2 dried red chilies
  • 1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • 1-2 sprigs curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps:

  1. Prepare Rice: Wash and soak the basmati rice for 20 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Tempering: Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds, mustard seeds, dried red chilies, curry leaves, and asafoetida. Sauté for 30 seconds until the mustard seeds splutter.
  3. Add Cashews and Green Chilies: Add cashews or peanuts and green chilies. Fry until the nuts turn golden brown.
  4. Cook Rice: Add the drained rice to the pan and stir well. Add turmeric powder and salt. Sauté for a couple of minutes.
  5. Add Coconut: Add the fresh grated coconut and mix well. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  6. Cook the Rice: Add water, bring to a boil, and then reduce the heat. Cover the pan and cook for 15-20 minutes or until the rice is cooked and water is absorbed.
  7. Serve: Garnish the coconut rice with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with raita or curry.

Tip: You can add a few fried onions for extra flavor and texture!
